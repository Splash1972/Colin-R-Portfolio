About Me

I am a professional drummer of over 35 years, currently employed by Cirque Du Soleil in Orlando, and formerly a full-time drummer for Blue Man Group of 15 years, ending only as a casualty of the pandemic. I am a teacher at Windermere Preparatory School, both for private lessons and as a teacher’s assistant. I am fluent with Pro Tools as well, and have written several cues for TV including the following shows: The Bachlorette, All Girls’ Garage, To The Rescue, and several others as I play other instruments as well.
